    Welcome to Magic The Gathering!

    Welcome to Magic The Gathering here at Iowa State! If you would like to learn how to play Magic The Gathering feel free to come down to one of our meetings! We are always looking for new players.

    Our Mission Statement

    This club is dedicated to growing a healthy community of Magic: The Gathering players at Iowa State. We seek to foster and cater to new and old players in a healthy environment. Our mission is to promote casual and competitive card gaming in a positive, inclusive, and diverse community.
