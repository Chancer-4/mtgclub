    • Online Resources for Magic: The Gathering

    Below are a list of resources helpful to those starting the game or for vetarans who need info about the next hot deck!

      Channel Fireball is one of the oldest, and well-known content sites for Magic: The Gathering. Started in 2012 by Luis Scott Vargas, a well-known professional player, the site has grown in popularity ever since. A great place for good information about the current meta, as well as good source of podcasts about Magic The Gathering play theory.


      MTGStocks is the best source to keep up-to-date on card prices about Magic: The Gathering hands down.


      Commander is one of the most popular formats of Magic right now, and what better way to research and learn about it than through EDHREC. It is an extremely helpful database cataloging hundreds of thousands of decks from a multitude of websites all in one easy to use interface. Browsing the website is a breeze as well.


      MTGtop8 is the best database of Tournament data decks. Whether you are looking for Modern decks, Standard decks, or Legacy decks. This place has all of that and more.
